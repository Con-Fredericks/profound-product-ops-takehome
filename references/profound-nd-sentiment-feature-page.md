# AI Search Brand Sentiment Analysis Tool

## Control How AI Talks About Your Brand

Profound's brand sentiment analysis tool shows whether AI is portraying a brand positively or negatively, which narratives are gaining ground, and what sources are shaping the story.

## Core Capabilities (as stated on the page)

- **Dedicated Sentiment Prompts** - Generate prompts designed to extract how AI evaluates a brand on specific topics.
- **Sentiment Dashboard & Charts** - Positive, negative, and trending sentiment in one view with daily tracking.
- **Key Themes & Root Cause Analysis** - Find recurring narratives shaping how AI describes a brand and drill into the sources behind them.
- **Sentiment-to-Action Workflows** - Turn sentiment data into content and messaging improvements.

## Measure Brand Sentiment Across AI Search

Profound tracks sentiment at the prompt level, broken down by topic, tag, and platform.

- **Dedicated Sentiment Prompts:** explicit evaluative prompts (how a brand is perceived, what it's known for, what buyers say, how it compares to competitors).
- **Topics & Tags:** applied to sentiment prompts to organize by theme and isolate where sentiment is strong, eroding, or trending negative.
- **Sentiment Dashboard & Charts:** aggregated sentiment score with a positive/negative breakdown by prompt, topic, and platform, with trend lines over time.
- **Key Themes & Patterns:** a ranked list of recurring AI narratives about the brand, with mention counts, trend direction, and associated source URLs.

## Diagnose Root Causes and Act on Sentiment Data

- **Root Cause Investigation:** drill into any theme to see the specific AI evaluations and publishers, pages, product reviews, or competitor claims driving it.
- **Perception vs. Reality Analysis:** distinguishes perception issues (content narrative is wrong, product is fine) from reality issues (AI is reflecting a genuine product gap).
- **Sentiment Trend Monitoring:** daily tracking across all prompts and platforms to catch negative narratives early or confirm content/outreach efforts are shifting perception.
- **Actionable Workflows:** use theme data to brief content teams, feed negative themes into PR outreach, or route insights to product teams.

## FAQ Highlights (as stated on the page)

- AI forms views on a brand from what it reads across the web; Profound shows which URLs/publishers drive positive or negative themes so a company can prioritize interventions (publish content, contact publishers, build citable owned content).
- Sentiment prompts are configured alongside standard tracking prompts but are designed to explicitly ask AI to evaluate or describe the brand.
- Negative themes are prioritized by frequency and trend direction; Root Cause analysis then identifies whether driving sources are influenceable (earned media, PR, review sites) or structural (real product issues).
- Sentiment data can feed directly into Agents to generate content targeting specific narratives.
- Recommended monitoring cadence is weekly as a baseline, with a 48-72 hour check after major PR or content events; Profound's underlying tracking cadence is daily.

No architecture, model, scoring formula, or classification methodology is disclosed anywhere on this page.
